Drinking Coffee Take a cup of coffee Caffeine is known to inhibit receptors in the brain for the chemical adenosine. The researchers found that these receptors also control the negative effects of chronic stress and that stress-induced behaviour can be reversed by blocking the receptors.

Drink your morning coffee or during your busy day, this can be your source of energy, soothe and focus your mind.

Reminder:
Don't drink too much coffee, you can drink 1-2 cup a day.
